Economic Outlook 2025: Opportunities & Challenges

Aired: June 15, 2025 | Duration: 45:32

0:00 45:32

Episode Description

In this special edition, our experts analyze the economic outlook for 2025. We discuss the challenges and opportunities facing the African economy, with a focus on Cameroon. Our guests include Dr. Marie Ngassa, economist at the University of Yaoundé, and Mr. Jean Paul, director of the National Investment Agency.

Key Topics:
  • Economic growth projections for Central Africa
  • Impact of global markets on local economies
  • Investment opportunities in emerging sectors
  • Government policies and their economic impact
